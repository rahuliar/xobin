Frequently Asked Question

What is meant by “Upto 50 Simultaneous test takers” ?

It means at a given time a maximum of 50 candidates can be taking the test. The 51st candidate will have to wait for some one to finish a test before she can start. You will be able to monitor all the candidates who are taking the test in the Live feed in your dashboard.
